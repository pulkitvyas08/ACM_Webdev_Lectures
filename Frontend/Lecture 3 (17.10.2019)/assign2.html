22ND JULY 2019

Dearest all,

A College Is A New Beginning In Any Students Life. After The Much Disciplined Life Of Schools There Comes The College. It Gives Us A Sense Of Responsibility As Well As Freedom. We Suddenly Start Thinking That Woo We Are Actually Grown Up Wand That Now We Can Do Anything And Everything. We Feel Like A Free Bird.My first day at college had a great meaning to me. It was the beginning of my dream to go to college and to pursue a higher education.I had so many emotions running trough me , it was the first time being in this level and the first time paying for education myself. I always wanted to be a college student, a serious student who would decide what to be in her life. The first year of my freshman year and Fall semester. That day I had so many things on my mind, from what I was going to wear to figure out where the class number and the floor it would be located at. It was actually a day full of new experiences. Everyone was very excited and meeting people from different parts of the country. And getting to know the culture and tradition of various people.
It was also the day when many people were alloted their dorm rooms, and meeting their fellow roommates.

So yeah...it was a great day. And then on usual daily routine and fun as well as studies continued on....

Thanking You,
